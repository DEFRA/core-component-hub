Contribute a Component

Help other teams by sharing your reusable components. Follow this guide to propose adding your component to the Hub.

Before you start

Your component should meet these criteria:

  • It's reusable across multiple Defra services or teams
  • It follows GDS and Defra design and development standards
  • It has clear documentation and examples
  • Your team can commit to maintaining it
  • It's accessible (WCAG 2.1 AA compliant)

What you need to provide

Component information

  • Name: Clear, descriptive name for your component
  • Description: Brief explanation of what it does and its benefits
  • Category: Platform, Forms, Maps, Authentication, Payments, Communications, or other
  • Status: Active, Beta, or Deprecated

Team information

  • Owner: Team or individual responsible for the component
  • Contact: Email address for support and questions
  • Commitment: Confirmation your team will maintain the component

Documentation

  • Documentation link: URL to comprehensive documentation
  • Repository link: URL to source code repository
  • Examples: Working examples or demo applications
  • Getting started guide: How teams can start using your component

How to submit

Simply submit a pull request to the core-component-hub repository.

Ongoing responsibilities

Once your component is in the Hub, you'll need to:

  • Keep documentation up to date
  • Respond to support requests from other teams
  • Update the component status every 6 months
  • Notify us of any major changes or if the component becomes deprecated
